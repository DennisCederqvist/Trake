        What is Trake

        What happens when you grab the first globaly recognized mobile game ever made SNAKE (1997) by Nokia and the much more obscure arcade game of TRON: Light Cycles (1982) by Bally Midway and smooch them together?
        Thats right you get TRon/snAKE = TRAKE!

        Trake is a school project made by Dennis C for ChasAcademy, to learn the basics of how to use LLM's during the workprocess and how to implement some simple online/multiplayer functionality. However, the first part went very well, the second part tho... Well, let's just say, it could have gone better.
        But hey, it got done!
